Submerge Magazine

Logo & Editorial Design

Submerge is a modern, young-adult oriented music magazine that focuses on Canadian indie and alternative artists. I designed the logo, cover design, and spreads. The goal of the magazine is to shoutout current events and artists to provide exposure to those who are not yet widely discovered.

This was such a fun project because I absolutely love indie and alternative music - especially the obscure, and especially Canadian. When looking around, I noticed there aren't any magazines that focus on indie music Canada-wide. I thought it was a good opportunity to make this magazine exactly that, and show off what our Canadian favourites are up to.

I fully designed an 8x10" 68 page magazine for print and ePub with the articles and photos cited.
