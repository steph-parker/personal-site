About me

I freaking love pizza

I could eat pepperoni pizza for the rest of my life and be satisfied. Hot and ready, home-made, or frozen - I’ll never complain. Except if you put pineapple on your pizza... THAT is a true crime

Cats are the best

I have a 4-year-old tortiouseshell cat named Amira. She is both the devil and an angel simultaneously. Most of the time she’s in my lap while I work

I was made for design

I’ve been illustrating and writing my own stories since I was 3. In high school, I took a career path test and scored a 97% match for Graphic Designer. I can’t imagine doing anything else

Raised in Tillsonburg

My back still aches when I hear that word. Yes, the place from the Stompin Tom song. I grew up there and man is it boring. I’m now based in the Niagara area, and it’s been a blast so far

Niagara College grad

I went to Niagara College from 2019-2022 and did the craziness that is online school for a year during the pandemic. Weird at first, but it became so normal and really enhanced my time management skills. AND I got to spend more time with my cat

Worked for NC

May 2021 to April 2022, I worked as an Associate Graphic Designer in marketing at Niagara College. I got to work with exceptional graphic designers as a team to produce print and digital media. Super fun experience and wonderful people
