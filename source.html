Source Yogurt Sustainable Redesign

Package Design

Yogurt packages are one of the thousands of plastic products polluting the Earth today. For this redesign, the goal was to make the new packaging as sustainable and accessible as possible.

Instead of Yoplait's plastic cartons, I chose a reusable glass jar as the container. The labels are made with seed paper and vegetable inks which are the safest for the enviornment. After use, the seed paper can be planted in soil to grow plants!

Inclusive design and accessability were considered, as the patterns make the designs easy to differentiate for those who are colour-blind, and Braille is included for the visually impaired.
