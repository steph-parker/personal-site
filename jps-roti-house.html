JP's Roti House

Brand Identity

JP’s Roti House is an authentic Caribbean and West Indian restaurant based in Welland, Ontario. Since 2018, they’ve been serving up their specialty; authentic roti and curry!

Junior, owner of JP’s Roti House, was in need of a new brand identity since changing the company name. The new logo needed to appeal to individuals 23+, and have a modern, friendly appearance.

I worked closely with Junior to develop a brand identity with his inspiration; the Rasta flag. The red, gold and green colours in the flag are represented here. I designed custom typography for the logo with emphasis on a hand-made feel to match the originality of his cuisine.
