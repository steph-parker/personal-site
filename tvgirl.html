TV Girl Album Art

Album Art Illustration

TV Girl is an American indie band who makes hypnotic pop music. As a fan of their music, I chose to redesign the album art for their 2014 album "French Exit" in my illustration style.

I started by listening to the all 12 tracks and writing down specific visual words I thought of as ideas for potential aspects of the illustration. "Boom box", "cityscape", and "walking down the street" were three ideas that stood out to me. Then I sketched 8 concepts using those themes, which narrowed down to one.

I took that final concept and digitally fleshed it out to three different compositions. All three were strong but the one I chose in the end really captures the fun, groovy vibe of the album.
